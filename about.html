About Us

Nestled near the shores of Lake Eyasi in Tanzania, Sonayi Safari Lodge & Campsite offers a perfect blend of comfort, culture, and adventure. Our lodge is located close to the traditional Hadzabe and Datoga tribes, giving you a unique opportunity to explore their fascinating history and way of life.

Whether you choose to stay in our well-furnished rooms, luxury tents, or experience open-air camping under the stars, we ensure top-quality hospitality and authentic Tanzanian warmth.

We offer cultural tours, wildlife safaris, bird watching, and adventure experiences, all tailored to give you a memory of a lifetime.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional safari and cultural experiences while promoting sustainable tourism and preserving local heritage.

Our Vision

To be the leading eco-friendly safari lodge in Tanzania, recognized globally for our service, cultural respect, and environmental care.
